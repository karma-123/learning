ABOUT US

Duedroi Rangwang Zhidhey Tshogchung ( DRZT) Animal Rights and Welfare Association (ARWA) is CSOA registered Tshogpa under registration No. CSOA/PBO-46, PO Box 920, Thimphu.

The Duedroi Rangwang Zhidhey Tshogchung ( DRZT) is a non-profit organization established to cultivate compassion for animals by promoting the abstention from consuming all animal meat related products, so as to benefit all sentient beings, irrespective of any religion or caste; to advise and support in the development and promotion of vegetarianism.

We educate people to lead life in accordance with truth and high morality at all levels that affect our spiritual, physical and mental development. This noble and sustainable concept was initially born in the hearts of group of merciful people who initiated the creation of a Wechat group to save ill fated animals from the butchers. The DRZT was formed in mid 2015 in dedication to the 60th Birth Annualsary of the 4th Druk Gyalpo, the founding father of GNH for all sentient beings. The DRZT Foundation Day is fixed on 11th of November coinciding with His Majesty the Fourth King’s birth Anniversary and the Children’s Day.

The DRZT has 7 trustee board members with over 10,386 members in 20 Dzongkhags, USA, Dubai and Australia who are guided by the DRZT Article of Associations, Service Rules and Regulations, Financial Manual, and Memorandum of Association of the Civil Society Organization Authority.

Aims & Objectives


The Association is formed to promote peaceful life for all sentient beings, without imposing but through persuasion:

a. To promote ways and means to adopt a Karzay lifestyle

b. To strive to avoid or reduce ways and means of a Marzay lifestyle

c. To emphasize the building of worthy relationships between human beings and the four elements of earth, water, fire and wind (Djung-wa-Zhi)

d. To disseminate the benefits of non-violence and a lifestyle of vegetarianism that does not demand killing of animals

e. To cultivate good intention and knowledge in human beings through disseminating of information and promotion.

Scope of the Activities


The scope of the activities covers wide range of topics such as Karzay (vegetarian), lifestyle, Advocacy programmes, conducting training on vegetable culinary, designing and distribution of advocacy materials such as pamphlet/ brochures and posters. Observation of World Vegetarian Day, DRZT Foundation Day and Development of Vegetarian data base are the main aims and objectives of the Association.

Since the functions of the Association is intended to promote peace, love and affection to animals and society at large, the Association and its members will use any measures to stop bloodshed and killing of animals.

However, the Association will never coerce any individual to follow the Association and its instructions but will persuade to follow its recommendations, suggestions, practices lead by example. The Association will help everyone and seek their supports, cooperation as the public dissemination will be the main focus of activities.

ORGANOGRAM



ACTIVITIES


Foundation Day

We observed 1st foundation day on 11th of November 2019 which will be observed regularly from now on. The foundation day is fixed coinciding with His Majesty the Fourth King’s birth anniversary and the children’s day aimed to pay our tribute and respect to the father of Gross National Happiness, who brought peace and happiness in our country for all sentient being to enjoy happiness and freedom.


Board Meeting

The board meeting shall be held after every 6 months once before the Annual General Meeting . The quorum for trustee members shall be one-third of the total members.


Areas of Operation

In principle the Association does not have any physical or geographical limit since it is intended to help every sentient being regardless of any limitations. However, for the purpose of legal recognition and operational purpose, which will mainly function within the territorial boundary of the Kingdom of Bhutan as per the law of the land.

Annual General Meeting (AGM)

The Annual General Meeting shall be forum comprising of board, donors and members to discuss on the issues pertaining to association’s affairs, review and endorse of annual accounts; elect new board members if necessary and discuss other important issues submitted by donor and members for the improvement and benefit of the association.
The Annual General Meeting shall be held once in every year which shall be chaired by the Chairman / Dy. Chair. The members may submit points required to discuss in Annual General Meeting to the Executive Director in writing well in advance.
The ED shall serve notice to all the members regarding Annual General Meeting at least one month in advance with date, time and venue. The quorum for AGM is one-third of the total members including registered members and legitimate donors. The 1st Annual General Meeting was held on 26th October 2019 where more than 180 members from 20 Dzongkhags have attended the meeting.

Members & beneficiaries

The Association does not have any specific beneficiaries and there is no direct benefits provided to anyone. It will help every sentient being (including human beings, animals, insects, etc).
However, we have 7 members board of trustees, senior staff and over 10,386 members affirm that incomes, earnings, movable or immovable properties of the society shall be solely utilized for the promotion of its aims and objectives as set forth in the Articles and Memorandum Association. The members will increase every time based and on interest and advocacy campaign etc.
